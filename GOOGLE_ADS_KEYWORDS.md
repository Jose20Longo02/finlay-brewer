## üéØ Keyword Strategy

### Match Types to Use:

1. **Phrase Match** (recommended for most)
   - Example: `"buy property nice france"`
   - Shows for searches that include this phrase
   - Good balance of control and reach

2. **Exact Match** (for high-value keywords)
   - Example: `[nice france real estate]`
   - Only shows for exact searches
   - Best for your top keywords

3. **Broad Match** (use sparingly)
   - Example: `buy property nice`
   - Shows for related searches
   - Can be expensive, use with caution

### Recommended Match Types:

**Exact Match (High Priority):**
- `[buy property nice france]`
- `[nice france real estate]`
- `[french riviera properties]`
- `[luxury villas nice france]`

**Phrase Match (Most Keywords):**
- `"properties for sale nice france"`
- `"nice france apartments"`
- `"international property buyers nice"`
- `"english speaking real estate nice"`

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Start with 15-20 keywords** - Don't add too many at once
2. **Focus on location-specific** - Always include "Nice" or "French Riviera"
3. **Target international buyers** - Include "international" or "english" keywords
4. **Use negative keywords** - Add terms like "rent", "cheap", "free" to avoid irrelevant clicks
5. **Monitor performance** - Pause low-performing keywords after 1-2 weeks
6. **Test and optimize** - Add new keywords based on search terms report
