## üìä Testing Strategy

1. **Start with Option A** for each title (marked as "Best")
2. **Add Option B** as alternative for A/B testing
3. **Monitor performance** after 1-2 weeks
4. **Pause low performers** and keep winners
5. **Test new variations** based on what works

---

## üéØ Pro Tips

- **Use 2-4 descriptions per ad** - Google will test and optimize
- **Match description to title** - Keep messaging consistent
- **Include location** - "Nice, France" or "French Riviera"
- **Highlight unique value** - English support, international focus
- **Clear call-to-action** - Tell users what to do next
- **Test different angles** - Service vs. property type vs. location
