## üí° Tips for Google Ads Setup

1. **Use 3 titles per ad** - Google will test and show the best performing ones
2. **Match titles to keywords** - If targeting "Nice properties", use titles with "Nice"
3. **Test different angles** - Location, service, property type, international focus
4. **Include location** - "Nice" or "French Riviera" in most titles
5. **A/B test** - Create multiple ad variations with different title combinations
6. **Monitor performance** - Pause low-performing titles after 1-2 weeks
