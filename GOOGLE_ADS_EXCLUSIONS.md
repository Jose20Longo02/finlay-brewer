## üîç How to Exclude Countries in Google Ads

### Step-by-Step:

2. **Click on "Locations" or "Geographic targeting"**
3. **Click "Exclude" or "Excluded locations"**
4. **Search for each country** from the list above
5. **Add them to exclusions**
6. **Save changes**

### Alternative Method:

1. **In location targeting section**
2. **Look for "Exclude locations" option**
3. **Add countries from exclusion list**
4. **Save**
